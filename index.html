About Me

Welcome to my personal webpage! I am Dr. Madhusmita Panda, a dedicated biologist and scientific data analyst with a rich background in cancer biology and genomics. Currently, I work part-time at Manuscriptome, where I apply my expertise in scientific data analysis. My professional journey includes a significant role as a Senior Genome Analyst at Medgenome Labs, Bangalore, where I specialized in genetic testing through next-generation sequencing technologies, focusing on various cancer types including breast, prostate, ovary, lung, and brain cancers.

I earned my Doctorate (Ph.D.) in Cancer Biology from the Central University of Tamil Nadu, Thiruvarur, Tamil Nadu. My research centered on the "Alterations in Oral Bacterial Communities Associated with Oropharyngeal, Hypopharyngeal, and Oral Cancer in Assam (North-East India)." I also hold an M.Sc. in Life Sciences from the National Institute of Technology (NIT), Rourkela, Odisha.

With a strong foundation in both wet and dry lab techniques, including DNA & RNA isolation, PCR, next-generation sequencing, biostatistics, and bioinformatics, I have contributed to numerous research articles and publications in the field of cancer genomics. My passion for teaching also sees me guiding students for NEET preparation and teaching biology to students from classes 8th to 12th at Paritran Classes.
